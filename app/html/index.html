Accounts

  • To set up a new account click the button above. You can create as many accounts as you want. Think of them like email addresses.

    Start by choosing a nickname, or handle, for your account. Handles should be short, and must NOT have a @ character in them. iotalk will generate a unique tag for the account. The account address will be a combination of the handle and tag and will look similar to an email address, something like myhandle@FJKEJISENRNDKRMTNYJSKWMTNYT


  • Use the button above to add contacts. Enter a valid iotalk address.
